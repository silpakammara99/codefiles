Ground Discussion:- 

A group of people talking about a topic is called group discussion.

Do's:-

1.initiate yourself

2.listen to other participants

3. maintain eye-contact

4. GIve others time to speak

5. Disagreement in a polite way

6. Be confident

7. In conclusion we have to give good suggestion.

Don'ts:-

1. Don't use judgement/argument language

2. Don't be aggressive

3. Don't interrupt others

4. Don't focus on only one participant

5. Don't give more negatives of the topic.
